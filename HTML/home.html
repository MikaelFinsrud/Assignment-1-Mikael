What is Astrobit?

Astrobit is a fast-paced online multiplayer top-down shooter where you create your own spaceships by combinining weapons, abilities and other components.
You bring your awesome self-crafted spaceships into epic battles against other players. Astrobit tests your shooter skills, but also allows you to unleash your creativity when creating your spaceships!
